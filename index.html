Welcome to Rayville First Assembly of God. We Would like to thank you for having an entrust in our church. We would like you to come by and join us. Our service is on Sunday, at 10AM and Wendnesday, at 8PM.


Meet the Pastor


Mike and Beverly Jackson have been the pastors of Rayville First Assembly since September 11, 2017. They are natives of Oregon but have lived in the South for several years, most recently in South Carolina. Both are alumni of Northwest College in Kirkland, Washington, and Beverly also attended the University of South Carolina. They have four children, nine grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Mike likes history, genealogy and music and is a dedicated walker. Beverly is an exceptional cook and is known for her beautiful quilts. She is also an accomplished pianist. She and Mike dated while singing in a gospel group and have been able to do considerable traveling. The foundation of their marriage and ministry is their total commitment to God and desire to disciple others.

Mission

The Mission of Raville Assembly of God is to serve Christ Jesus, in the preaching,and teaching we do. Sending and supporting missionaries thoughtout the world. Help Christians keep their walk of faith through discipleship.
